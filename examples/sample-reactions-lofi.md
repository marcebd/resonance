## What this demonstrates

Same variant. Same brief. Two listeners produce a 2-point gap in saveWorthiness — but the **structural disagreement is in the reasoning**, not just the score: Søren reads the unresolved seventh as "genuinely smart" load-bearing tension; Adaeze reads it as "low-grade anxious hum" that almost works but doesn't. Both correctly cite the same musical detail; their personas' stated traits produce opposite interpretations.

Note the transmission graph also converges on Dagny scoring 3 from both Søren and Adaeze — independent panel members agree she's the wrong audience for *this specific* variant, even though they disagree about its quality for themselves. That kind of cross-validated audience signal is what the synthesis stage works with.
